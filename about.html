About Trivial Math

Our Mission

Trivial Math is a personal project born from a passion for making mathematics clear, accessible, and intuitive. This website serves as a digital garden of curated notes and study materials designed to support students, professionals, and lifelong learners on their mathematical journey.

What You'll Find

The notes library is actively growing and currently focuses on core undergraduate and graduate topics, including:

  • Real & Complex Analysis
  • Abstract & Linear Algebra
  • Topology
  • Numerical Methods
  • Programming tools like NumPy and LaTeX
